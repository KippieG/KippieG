## 🧠 What I Do

I turn complex, ambiguous business problems into structured requirements, clean processes, and products that actually get built and used.

My edge over most BAs: I have both **business analysis discipline** and **real development experience**. I've shipped macOS and iOS apps, built databases, written automation scripts, and run a company. That means I write requirements engineers can actually work from — and I catch what's technically unfeasible before it becomes a 3-sprint detour.

```
✅  I understand stakeholders AND developers — I bridge both
✅  I've shipped real products — I know what's technically feasible
✅  I've managed clients and accounts — I know what stakeholders actually want
✅  I've run my own company — I understand business trade-offs and priorities
✅  I write BRDs, BPMNs, user stories, and acceptance criteria that hold up
✅  Dutch (native) · French (fluent) · English (professional)
```

> *"A good BA doesn't just document requirements — they make sure the right problem gets solved."*
