## 🧠 How I Think

I'm not someone who waits. If I want to know how something works, I figure it out. If I think something should exist, I try to build it. I don't have a formal education in any of this — I learned by doing, breaking things, and doing it again.

That approach has costs (slow at first, lots of dead ends) and benefits (no theoretical constraints, just real-world feedback).

I value:
- **Clarity** — in code, in communication, in decisions
- **Execution** — shipping beats theorizing, every time
- **Honesty** — about what I know, what I don't, and what something actually takes
